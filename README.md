## 🛠 Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install the APK on your Android device:

1. **Download**: Click the download link above to get the `.apk` file.
2. **Enable Unknown Sources**: 
   - Open **Settings** on your Android device.
   - Go to **Security** or **Privacy**.
   - Look for **Install unknown apps** or **Unknown sources** and toggle it on for your browser or file manager.
3. **Install**: Open the downloaded `.apk` file from your notification bar or file manager and tap **Install**.
4. **Launch**: Once installed, find the **HU Story** icon in your app drawer and start connecting with your campus!

## ✨ Features

- **Rants**: Share your thoughts anonymously or publicly.
- **Spots**: Discover the best study and chill zones on campus.
- **Events**: Stay updated on all campus happenings.
- **Projects**: Find teammates and collaborate on student projects.
